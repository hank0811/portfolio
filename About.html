The Person

Bridging AI Logic with Human-Centered Design.

I am a designer and B.Tech student who specializes in the intersection of technical architecture and visual simplicity. My background in Java and AI analytics allows me to understand the "how" behind the products I design.

Whether I am building brand designs or crafting high-fidelity UI prototypes, my goal is to strip away unnecessary noise until only the essential user experience remains.

"Design is the result of controlled discipline."

My process is heavily influenced by the philosophy of discipline. I draw inspiration from a few set of people I follow and applying that same level of "controlled aggression" to solve complex design problems.
